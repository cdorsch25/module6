Summary of a Woman

Claire Dorsch is a 25 year-old student at IU Indianapolis. She enjoys listening to Lizzy McAlpine, Rainbow Kitten Surprise, and Hozier. Claire will do almost anything for a fresh chocolate croissant, but don't tempt her with a good time. When she is not at work or at school, she is watching a movie, becoming obsessed with one of the actors, then watching every piece of media they have ever been a part of. You will find her with her e-reader in hand, matcha in the other, and too many things in her Trader Joe's tote bag.
